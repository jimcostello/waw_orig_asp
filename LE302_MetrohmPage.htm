Join us and learn about a brand new fully integrated technology that has every lab chemist excited! This free webinar will introduce the theory of combustion IC and present applications for various matrices across different industries.

Key points of discussion will be:

• Introduction of the first fully integrated Combustion IC for simultaneous determination of
halogens and sulfur in petroleum products, polymers, flame retardants, textiles, special
chemicals, catalysts and more

• Discover new growth opportunities for both industrial and contract labs

• Find out about the global halogen free products initiative and how combustion IC fits in

• Get free training on existing methods that use combustion IC (ASTM D7359-08, UOP991-11,
ASTM D5987-96 (2007) and more)

• Learn about new and upcoming regulations (ASTM, DIN EN, IEC RoHS WEEE, etc.) that make combustion IC more relevant than ever before.
